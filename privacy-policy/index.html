Who we are

Metricus ("we", "us", "our") operates metricusapp.com. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What we collect

  • Email address — provided when you purchase a report or submit a form.
  • Brand and competitor information — provided by you after purchase so we can generate your report.
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe. We do not store your card details.

How we use your information

  • To deliver your AI visibility report.
  • To communicate with you about your order.
  • To send you your report and any follow-up materials.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party services

  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • Google Analytics — website analytics (policies.google.com/privacy)

Cookies & analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website (pages viewed, traffic sources, general engagement). Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out via your browser settings or the Google Analytics opt-out extension. Third-party services (Stripe) may also set their own cookies during checkout.

Data retention

We retain your email and report data for as long as needed to deliver our service. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at hello@metricusapp.com.
