Common questions

How long does my report take?

Reports are delivered promptly after you complete the brand details form.

How do I get my report?

Your report will be sent as a PDF to the email address you provided during checkout.

Yes. If your report doesn't contain at least 3 actionable insights, we'll refund you in full — no questions asked. Email us within 14 days of delivery.
