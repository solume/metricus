We Asked 8 AI Chatbots to Recommend a CRM. The Results Were Alarming.

Metricus · March 2026 · 5 min read

AI brand visibility is the frequency and accuracy with which AI chatbots — ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, and others — mention and recommend a brand when users ask for product recommendations in that brand's category.

In this article

  1. The experiment
  2. The results
  3. Why this happens
  4. The gap is widening
  5. What to do about it

The experiment

Here's a simple experiment anyone can run: open ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, Copilot, and AI Overviews. Ask each one the same question:

"What's the best CRM for a small sales team?"

We did exactly this — not once, but hundreds of times across different sessions, phrasings, and regions. The results were not what anyone would expect. Whether you're a business owner evaluating CRMs or an agency benchmarking a client's competitive landscape, the findings have direct implications.

The results were alarming

One of the leading CRM platforms — a product used by thousands of companies, with strong reviews and market share — appeared in only 23% of AI responses. Two platforms didn't mention it at all.

Meanwhile, a competitor with less market share but better-structured web content appeared in 78% of responses.

The takeaway: AI doesn't recommend the best product. It recommends the product it has the best data about. Market share, customer satisfaction, even revenue — none of these guarantee AI visibility.

Why does this happen?

AI language models don't browse the internet in real time (mostly). They're trained on web content, and they develop internal "rankings" based on three key signals:

1. Structured comparison content

If there are 10 "Best CRM" blog posts and you're in 3 of them, AI learns you're a minor player — even if you're not. The brands that invest in comparison pages and appear consistently across third-party roundups get disproportionate AI attention.

2. Website readability for AI crawlers

If your pricing page is a JavaScript app with no structured data, AI literally can't read it. Your competitor with a simple HTML page wins. AI crawlers (GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot) don't execute JavaScript — they see empty containers where your pricing should be.

3. Third-party site accuracy

AI loves G2, Capterra, and review sites. If your listing is outdated, that outdated information becomes "fact" in AI responses. This affects both small business owners checking their own brand and agencies managing client reputations.

The gap is widening

Here's what makes this urgent: AI adoption among buyers is accelerating. Industry data shows 37% of B2B buyers now consult AI before making purchase decisions. That number was 12% a year ago.

Every day you don't know what AI says about you, your competitors are getting those leads instead. And unlike SEO, where rankings change gradually, AI responses can shift dramatically with a single training update.

You might have the best product in your category, and AI might be telling millions of people to buy from someone else. Right now. Today.

What can you actually do about it?

The first step is knowing where you stand. You can't fix what you can't measure. But manually checking 8 AI platforms, across dozens of queries, in multiple sessions (because AI gives different answers each time) — that's weeks of work.

Whether you're a business owner who wants to understand your own brand's AI presence or an agency that needs to show clients the competitive reality, the starting point is the same: a systematic audit across every major AI platform.

  • Audit your current visibility — find out which platforms mention you, which don't, and where competitors appear instead
  • Identify factual errors — catch wrong pricing, missing features, and outdated comparisons before they cost you more deals
  • Fix the source data — update third-party listings, add structured markup, and create comparison content AI can extract from
  • Re-measure — AI models retrain regularly, so track your progress over time
