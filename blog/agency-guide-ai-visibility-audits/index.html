Agency Guide to AI Visibility Audits: White-Label Reports, Pricing and Tools

Metricus · Last updated: April 2026

AI visibility audits are emerging as a high-margin service line for digital marketing agencies. With brands increasingly asking why they don't appear in ChatGPT and other AI assistants, agencies that can deliver data-driven AI visibility reports are commanding premium engagement fees.

Why Agencies Are Adding AI Visibility Audits

The conversation in every client meeting has shifted. Alongside SEO rankings and paid search performance, brands now ask a question most agencies weren't prepared for: "Why doesn't ChatGPT recommend us?" This is not a niche concern from early adopters. A growing share of B2B and B2C buyers now consult AI assistants during purchasing research, and when a brand is absent from those answers, clients notice. Metricus AI visibility report data shows that brands visible in AI responses capture consideration before a prospect ever reaches a search engine. Agencies that can answer the AI visibility question retain clients who would otherwise look elsewhere for expertise.

AI visibility audits also represent a genuinely new revenue stream with strong margins. Unlike SEO retainers that require months of ongoing work, an AI visibility audit is a one-time deliverable: a structured report with findings, source analysis, and a prioritized action plan. You deliver it, bill for it, and move on. There is no dashboard to maintain, no monthly monitoring cost eating into your margin, and no platform subscription to manage per client. The deliverable model fits how agencies actually operate — project-based, client-facing, and cost-conscious.

The third reason is structural: clients don't want another dashboard login. They want a document with clear findings and specific recommendations they can act on. An AI visibility report is that document. It turns a vague "we're invisible in AI" concern into a concrete optimization roadmap with source maps, error audits, and ranked action items. That is the kind of deliverable you can present in a strategy meeting and bill at your standard consulting rate.

The Agency Economics

Agencies typically deliver AI visibility audits as $1,500–$3,000 engagements. The engagement includes your strategic analysis, client presentation, and recommendations layered on top of the underlying report data. Because the report itself is a one-time cost with no recurring subscription, the margin structure is straightforward and favorable.

Compare this to the alternative: per-client monthly subscriptions to AI monitoring dashboards that run $189–$300 per client per month, require analyst time to interpret, and still need to be translated into a client-facing deliverable. For an agency with ten clients, that is $22,000–$36,000 per year in tooling costs alone. The per-report model eliminates that overhead entirely.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do agencies deliver AI visibility audits to clients?

Agencies order a per-client AI visibility report, receive a complete deliverable with findings and recommendations, and present it to the client as part of a consulting engagement. No subscription or platform onboarding required.

What margins can agencies expect on AI visibility audits?

Agencies typically deliver AI visibility audits as $1,500–$3,000 engagements. The exact margin depends on how you package the deliverable with your strategic analysis and recommendations.

Do I need technical staff to offer AI visibility audits?

No. The report is generated for you with complete findings, source maps, error audits, and a prioritized action plan. Your role is adding the strategic context and client relationship layer.
