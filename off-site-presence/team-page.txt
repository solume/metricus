About Metricus

[Subhead]

[Body]
Metricus was created by a small team of search strategists, data engineers, and AI researchers who kept hearing the same question from clients: "What does ChatGPT say about us?"

Nobody had a good answer. The existing tools were either subscription-locked, limited to one or two platforms, or built for enterprises with six-figure budgets. We wanted something simpler: query the AI, analyze the output, deliver the insight. One report, one price.

That's Metricus.

[Section: What we believe]

AI is the new front page.
When someone asks ChatGPT for "the best CRM for small teams" and your product isn't in the answer, you've lost that customer before they ever visited your site. This is the new competitive landscape, and most brands don't even know they're invisible.

Reports should be affordable and honest.
We charge $49-$349 per report because we think visibility data shouldn't require a $500/month subscription. If our report doesn't deliver at least 3 actionable insights, we refund you. No questions.

Data beats opinion.
Every claim in a Metricus report is backed by actual AI outputs across 8 platforms and 200+ query variations. We show you exactly what was said, where, and why.

[Section: Our methodology]
We query ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Gemini, AI Overviews, Grok, DeepSeek, and Microsoft Copilot with 200+ prompt variations tailored to your brand's category. We analyze mention frequency, sentiment, positioning, competitor presence, and source attribution. Reports are delivered as PDF within 24 hours.

Full methodology details: https://metricusapp.com/methodology/
