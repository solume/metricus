Metricus measures how AI represents businesses and makes that data actionable for everyone. One-time AI visibility reports from $99. Published methodology.

The team

Recommender systems
and AI researchers.

Metricus was founded by researchers whose work spans recommender systems, information retrieval, natural language processing, and applied machine learning. Before building Metricus, the team spent over a decade studying the mechanisms that determine what algorithmic systems surface — and what they bury.

When large language models became the new interface layer between consumers and businesses, we recognized the problem immediately: the same dynamics that govern search ranking and recommendation feeds now govern AI-generated answers.
