Top Agent Opportunities

High impact · Quick win

1. Tier-1 Support Ticket Automation

Zendesk's own ticket history (200K+ resolved tickets) is perfectly structured for a support agent. An AI agent could auto-resolve 40-50% of tier-1 tickets using existing knowledge base articles and past resolution patterns. Estimated savings: 8 FTE hours/day.

High impact · 2-4 weeks

2. Sales Follow-Up Automation

Salesforce CRM data shows average 3.2 day delay on follow-ups after demos. An agent connected to CRM + calendar could auto-draft personalized follow-ups within 2 hours of every demo, pulling in discussed features and pricing from call notes.

Medium impact · Quick win

3. Internal Knowledge Retrieval

Engineering and support teams spend ~8 hours/week searching Confluence and Notion for internal docs. An agent with access to both could answer "how do we handle X?" questions instantly, sourced from actual documentation.
