Executive Summary

Bank of America appears in 41% of AI-generated banking recommendations — behind Chase (72%) and ahead of Wells Fargo (38%). While ChatGPT and Perplexity consistently mention BofA, Gemini and Grok rarely recommend it for personal banking. AI frequently cites BofA's mobile app strength but incorrectly states there is no fee-free checking option (Advantage SafePass is fee-free with direct deposit). Additionally, AI overstates minimum balance requirements by 2-3x across platforms. Correcting these errors and strengthening content around key query clusters could push visibility above 60%.

Prioritized Action Plan

1.
Fix fee-free checking visibility
AI universally claims BofA has no free checking. A dedicated landing page with Schema.org markup for Advantage SafePass would give AI crawlers the structured data they need.
2.
Publish head-to-head comparison content vs. Chase
In "Chase vs BofA" queries, Chase is favored 3:1. BofA's own comparison content is buried in marketing pages. A direct, factual comparison page would give AI balanced citation material.
3.
Create structured data for product lineup
AI platforms pull outdated fee structures from third-party comparison sites. Adding JSON-LD to each product page would fix this at the source.
4.
Target "best bank for young adults" query cluster
BofA has student products but AI never mentions them. This is a high-volume, low-competition query group.
5.
Address Gemini and Grok blind spots
Two major platforms don't mention BofA at all. Specific content strategy to fix this.
