Executive Summary

Bank of America is highly mentionable and usually shortlisted for mainstream full-service banking, but it is rarely the first recommendation for fee-first checking queries. Across 8 AI engines, BofA appears in 54% of banking recommendations — behind Chase (71%) but ahead of Capital One (48%) and Ally (39%). AI consistently positions BofA as a strong option for relationship-oriented buyers who want branches, digital tools, and Merrill-linked rewards. However, for generic "best checking account" queries, AI favors Capital One, Chase, Ally and SoFi because those sites lead with "no fees / no minimums / no overdraft fees" — a story BofA's site doesn't tell as clearly. 3 factual errors found related to fee structure, overdraft policy, and the Preferred Rewards transition.

What AI Says About Bank of America

Their Erica AI assistant and mobile app are among the best, and the Preferred Rewards program gives meaningful perks if you maintain higher balances across BofA and Merrill. The downside: their everyday checking accounts still carry monthly fees unless waived, which puts them behind Capital One and Ally for pure simplicity."
Claude

Prioritized Action Plan

1.
Add a checking account chooser above the fold
AI struggles to differentiate SafeBalance, Advantage Plus, and Relationship Banking. A prominent chooser ("simple no-overdraft checking" vs "standard everyday" vs "relationship with rewards") would give AI clear category signals instead of forcing inference across three product pages.
2.
Win the fee story faster
Competitor pages lead with "no fees / no minimums / no overdraft fees." BofA should surface SafeBalance earlier and compare it directly with Advantage Plus and Relationship on one screen. Currently, the fee structure creates more cognitive load than pages that communicate simplicity upfront.
3.
Package the unique angle explicitly: branch + digital + Merrill
Branch access + strong digital servicing + Merrill-linked rewards is a combination no competitor matches. But the site doesn't frame this as the decisive reason to choose BofA — AI can't extract a positioning story that isn't explicitly stated.
4.
Reduce overdraft ambiguity between account types
Advantage Plus defaults to standard overdraft treatment with $10 item fees, while SafeBalance has none. This distinction is buried. AI currently generalizes "BofA charges overdraft fees" because the difference isn't visible early in the flow.
5.
Add concrete proof points above the fold
Rival pages show "70,000+ ATMs", "5,000 branches", "2-day early pay." BofA's scale is comparable but not surfaced. Adding concrete numbers (ATM/branch count, app stats, service metrics) gives AI extractable proof to cite.
