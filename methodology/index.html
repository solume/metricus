Methodology

What we measure, and
why it's hard.

Metricus measures AI visibility through a three-phase process: query generation, pattern recognition, and strategic output.

AI platforms give different answers every time. Ask ChatGPT the same question twice and you'll get two different brand recommendations. That's why a single query tells you nothing — and why we query each platform hundreds of times to find the real patterns underneath the noise.

01

What We
Measure

For every brand, we measure five things across major AI platforms: how often you're mentioned, where you rank against competitors, what AI gets factually wrong, which sources it pulls from, and whether your language matches what buyers actually ask. Each dimension requires hundreds of queries — because AI gives different answers every time.

Real User Interface Simulation

We query AI the way your customers do — through the same interfaces they use, not developer APIs.

Same question, different answers

AI gives different answers to the same question. That's why a single query tells you nothing — we run hundreds to find the pattern.

02

Why It's
Hard

AI answers are nondeterministic — the same question produces different recommendations each time. Some answers come from real-time web search, others from the model's training data. Each platform weighs different sources differently. A brand can be visible on one AI and invisible on another. That's why we query AI hundreds of times per report — to separate signal from noise.
