How it works

Three steps to clarity.

01 person

You share your company name, website, and the category you compete in. Takes 2 minutes.

02 troubleshoot

We do the work.

Metricus analyzes how your brand appears across the AI platforms that matter to your buyers. We find what AI says, what it gets wrong, which sources shape the answers, and how you compare to competitors.

Delivered to your inbox. Exact findings, wording mismatches, source maps, and a fix list ranked by impact. You know what to change and where to start.
